First Aid

Basic First Aid Instructions

Bleeding

  • Apply Firm Pressure Immediately.
  • Elevate Wound Above Heart Level.
  • Use Clean Gauze or Bandage Only.
  • Avoid Removing Embedded Objects.
  • Medical Help If Bleeding Persists.

Fractures

  • Stop Movement, Immobilize Affected Area.
  • Apply Cold Compress or Ice Wrap.
  • Monitor for Shock, Check Vital Signs.
  • Do Not Move or Walk on Fractured Limb.
  • Seek Immediate Medical Attention.

    • Burns

  • Cool Burn with Cold Water Immediately.
  • Remove Nearby Clothing and Jewellery.
  • Apply Clean, Non-Stick Dressing or Bandage.
  • Avoid Breaking Blisters or Applying Ointments.
  • Seek Medical Help If Burn is Severe or Large.

    • Sprains

  • Stop Activity Immediately, Rest Affected Area.
  • Apply Ice Pack or Cold Compress.
  • Use Elastic Bandage or Compression Wrap.
  • Avoid Aggravating or Moving Affected Area.
  • Seek Medical Help if Swelling Increases.

    • Choking

  • Encourage Person to Cough Immediately.
  • Perform Back Slaps if Coughing Fails.
  • Use Abdominal Thrusts if Slaps Fail.
  • Avoid Attempting to Remove Object Manually.
  • Call for Emergency Services If Persists.

    • Shock

  • Lay Person Down with Legs Elevated.
  • Loosen Tight Clothing Around Neck, Waist.
  • Keep Person Warm with Blanket or Coat.
  • Avoid Giving Fluids or Medications.
  • Call for Medical Help Immediately.

    • For additional guidance, please consult a medical professional.
